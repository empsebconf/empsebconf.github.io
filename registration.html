To attend EMPSEB 31, you must be a PhD student at the time of the conference and not have attended previous editions of EMPSEB.

All participants are to present their work in the form of a short talk with a slideshow presentation. In addition, participants are encouraged to present their work as a traditional poster or as a creative alternative presentation. There will be prizes for best performance in all three categories!

Abstract submission opens on December 1st!

ESEB is offering our participants the Conference Travel Award 2026 to support their travel expenses! Check your eligibility and how to apply here!
Remember, the deadline for applying for this travel grant is 30 January, 2026
If you plan to apply and avail the Conference Award 2026, you must submit your abstract with us by 15 January, 2026.
