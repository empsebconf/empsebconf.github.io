To attend EMPSEB 31 (8-12 June, 2026), you must be a PhD student at the time of the conference. We prioritise students who did not attend the previous edition of EMPSEB (EMPSEB 30).

All participants are to present their work as a short, 10-12-minute talk with a slideshow presentation. In addition, participants are encouraged to present their work as a traditional poster or as a creative alternative presentation. There will be prizes for best performance in all three categories!

The registration fee is €500. This fee covers the participants' stay at the AHORN Hotel for the duration of the meeting, most meals (including breakfasts, lunches, tea breaks, and dinners), and shuttle bus.
