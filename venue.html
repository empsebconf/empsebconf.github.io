We are pleased to welcome you to the AHORN Hotel Am Fichtelberg for EMPSEB 31, taking place from 8 to 12 June, 2026 in the scenic town of Oberwiesenthal.

Located in the Ore Mountains (Erzgebirge), Oberwiesenthal—Germany’s highest town—is a year-round destination for summer and winter activities and an inspiring setting for scientific exchange.

The hotel offers professional conference facilities alongside leisure amenities, including free access to the indoor pool, gym, cinema, and various activities, with a sauna available at an additional cost.

Catering includes buffet breakfast and dinner, a two-course lunch, and morning and afternoon coffee breaks. Two spacious halls host parallel EMPSEB 31 sessions, complemented by comfortable rooms and scenic mountain views ideal for networking and discussion.

How do I get there?

Chemnitz, Germany is the nearest big city to Oberwiesenthal (easily reachable via 1 hour trains from Dresden and Leipzig). An hourly bus (Bus 210) runs from Chemnitz Central Station.
Switch to Bus 411 at its last stop at Annaberg-Buchholz and get off at Kurort Oberwiesenthal.
You're already in the town! A 13 minute walk from your stop (or take Bus A / Bus 414 from there and get off directly at the venue in 5 minutes!) and you'll be here!
This trip would take you a little over 2 hours to complete.

For participants’ convenience, shuttle buses will be arranged to and from Leipzig Main Station (Leipzig Hbf) and Prague Václav Havel Airport, at the end and start of the meeting, respectively. This service starts and ends at the venue.

In the REGISTRATION FORM, available to you once your abstract is approved, please mark your preferences if you wish to avail these services. The cost is already covered in your registration fee. Further information regarding location and timings will be available by late May.
