The European Meeting for PhD students in Evolutionary Biology (EMPSEB) is an annual conference organised by and intended for doctoral students. Since its establishment in 1995, EMPSEB has been a valuable annual gathering for doctoral students from around the world.

This year, EMPSEB31 will take place at the X Hotel in Y, Germany, from Month Day to Day, 2026. The conference offers a platform for PhD students to share ideas, learn, and connect with peers and professionals in the field.
